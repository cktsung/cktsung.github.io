Research Field of Interest

  1. Data Science Extract data characteristics and construct a heterogeneous data convergence center, covering thematic fields such as environmental sensing, smart life sensing, production and manufacturing, and agricultural environment, and design high-throughput data access environments, value-added application services, and high-performance Event decision-making calculation mechanism to assist decision-makers or administrators to grasp the status of the current environment and events that may occur in the future.
  2. Combinatorial Optimization Find balance points in multiple decision-making aspects through Heuristic Algorithm, Greedy Algorithm, Machine Learning and other technologies, so that the results can be balanced in different aspects. Advantages.
  3. Game Theory Application With various equilibrium concepts as the core, the design of stable calculation methods can be achieved by using mechanism design techniques.

Implementation Field of Interest

  1. Service Virtualization With Web Technology as the core, open data formats are used as data communication interfaces, and software and hardware services are virtualized (Virtualized) into Web Services, allowing users or programs to Designers can access software and hardware services through the Internet.
  2. Data Visualization In the environment of the Internet of Things, a large amount of data will be continuously generated. Common application fields include production line data or system logs. Most of these data record normal actions, but their utilization value is low. When used It is worth paying attention to the characteristic values ​​generated when or before the event occurs. In addition, graphically presenting the characteristics of data is an indispensable measurement tool for observers.
  3. Decision Optimization When decisions are made, they are usually limited by a variety of conditions, which results in defects in different aspects and the need for tradeoffs. Common constraints include resource constraints (Resource Constraints) and computing time constraints (Time Constraints), the use of combinatorial optimization to achieve balanced decision-making results under different constraints can be achieved by relying on Artificial Intelligence Technology.
